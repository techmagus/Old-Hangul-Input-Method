삐ᄙᅵ삐너 한글 (Pilipino Hangeul) IME

This is a Unicode-compliant IME (Input Method Editor). It's recommended to learn 삐ᄙᅵ삐너 한글 (Pilipino Hangeul) by reading the project's page.

This is the standard 한글 (Hangeul) layout. The Latin letters were removed to avoid confusion. The pronunciation of 한글 (Hangeul) do not correspond to the Latin letters found on a keyboard.

Feel free to contribute, or fork, the 삐ᄙᅵ삐너 한글 (Pilipino Hangeul) IME project.
